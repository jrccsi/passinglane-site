🏅 Badge Definitions
15 badges across 4 tiers — here's exactly how to earn each one.
Common Earned early. Recognizes first steps and daily habits.
🟢
First Green Light
Awarded when you answer your first question correctly. Everyone starts here — this is your first win.
📖
Handbook Hero
Awarded when you complete every question in any single module. You've fully covered one topic from the CA Driver's Handbook.
🔥
On Fire
Awarded when you answer 10 questions correctly in a single practice session without stopping.
Uncommon Earned through consistency and skill. Takes real effort.
📚
Bookworm
Awarded when you bookmark 10 or more questions. You're paying attention to the ones that matter — and coming back to them.
🦉
Night Owl
Awarded when you complete a practice session after 10 PM. Late-night studier, we see you.
💪
Comeback Kid
Awarded when you answer a question correctly after previously getting it wrong. You learned from your mistake.
⚡
Speed Demon
Awarded when you complete a full module in under 3 minutes. Fast and accurate — that's the goal.
🎯
Sharpshooter
Awarded when you achieve 90% or higher accuracy in a single practice session of at least 20 questions.
Rare Earned through sustained effort over time. Most users won't have all of these.
📅
Month on Fire
Awarded when you maintain a 30-day study streak. Thirty days in a row — no breaks, no excuses.
✅
Perfect Run
Awarded when you complete a full module with 100% correct answers on the first try — no wrong answers, no retakes.
📆
Week Warrior
Awarded when you study every day for 7 consecutive days. A full week of daily practice.
🧠
Big Brain
Awarded when you reach 80% or higher mastery across all 13 categories simultaneously. You know the whole handbook cold.
Legendary The hardest to earn. Only the most dedicated users will hold all three.
💎
Flawless
Awarded when you pass a Permit Test Simulation with a perfect score — 46 out of 46 questions correct.
🪪
Licensed
Awarded when you report that you passed your real California DMV written test. This is what it's all for. Comes with the gold plate.
🥇
First in Class
Awarded when you reach #1 on your friends leaderboard. You've outscored everyone in your crew.

Good to know

  • Badges are permanent once earned — they can't be lost.
  • All badges are visible on your profile whether earned or not, so you always know what you're working toward.
  • The Licensed badge requires self-reporting your DMV pass — we trust you.
  • Badge names are translated in Spanish in the app, but the icons and tiers are the same.
