Spanish Support

Can I take the California permit test in Spanish?

Yes. The California DMV offers Spanish learning and testing resources, including Spanish sample knowledge tests and Spanish online learning options. Passing Lane also supports English and Spanish across the full app, including questions, explanations, study insights, and the licensing guide.

Where can I practice in Spanish?

The DMV publishes Spanish sample knowledge tests and offers online learning in Spanish. Passing Lane adds a complete Spanish study path with the same question categories and readiness tracking as the English version.

Is Spanish only for parents?

No. Many teens prefer English at school and Spanish at home, so a bilingual plan helps the whole family understand the same rules and next steps.

Can I switch languages?

Yes. Passing Lane lets you switch between English and Spanish from your profile, so you can study in the language that helps the rule make sense.

What should Spanish speaking families focus on?

Start with the official handbook topics, then use practice questions to learn DMV wording. Parents should also review permit documents, driver education, behind the wheel training, insurance, and provisional license rules.

Passing Lane was built for California families who need the permit path to be clear in both English and Spanish.
