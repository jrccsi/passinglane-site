Driver’s Education

If you’re under 17½, California requires 30 hours of DMV-approved driver’s education before you can apply for your permit. Online and in-person courses both qualify — as long as the course is state-approved.

What you need to know

  • Completion earns you Form OL 237 — required at the DMV to get your permit
  • Online courses cost $30–$80, classroom courses $150–$300
  • If you’re 17½ or older, driver’s ed is not required — skip to Step 2
  • Look for courses explicitly listed as DMV-approved — not all are
  • Keep a photo of your OL 237 in case the original is lost
