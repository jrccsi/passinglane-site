Step 3

Behind-the-Wheel Training

After getting your permit, California requires at least 6 hours of professional behind-the-wheel training with a licensed instructor before you can take the road test. Your instructor must sign your permit — no signature, no road test.

What you need to know

  • Minimum 6 hours of professional BTW training required by law
  • Typically done in three 2-hour sessions — spread out as needed
  • Instructor must sign your permit after at least 1 hour is complete
  • That signature is required before any supervised practice with a parent
  • Expect to pay $50–$100 per hour — shop around for packages
  • Make sure the school is licensed by the California DMV
